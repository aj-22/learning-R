Mental health is a pervasive issue that plagues the United States. People of all classes, gender, and race experience mental health issues.  We are looking at mental health from a substance consumption angle have decided to explore if increased in substance consumption impacts mental health in the United States using the NHANES data set.

Subtances under our scope:
Alcohol
Marijuana
Heroin
Cocaine
Meth

This project is work in progress.
